Tutoring : Les Cordées de la Réussite

During my first year at ENSEEIHT I was engaged in a programm called “Cordée de la Réussite” in which the objective is assisting student at middle school for a project called “Atelier Robotique”.

We were helping them programming a robot to perform simple tasks. At the end of the year, we participated in the junior robotics competition in "La cité de l'espace" in Toulouse.

Volonteering for Paris 2024 OG

During a summer I worked as a volunteer for Paris 2024 Olympic Games.. My role was to guide athletes, delegations and drivers of athletes’ buses in the welcoming zone of an Olympic competition site. This experience improved my competences in communicating in English and group working.
